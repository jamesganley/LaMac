About Us

La Macassar

Le Macassar is located on the main square of the town of Corbie, 15 minutes from the centre of Amiens – the capital of the Picardie region of northern France. By car we are 1.5 hours from Paris & Calais, and 2.5 hours from Brussels . The nearest airports are Paris Charles de Gaulle (<1.5 hours) and Paris Beauvais (<1 hour). As well as the local train station in Corbie, the high speed ‘TGV’ train station of Haut Picardie is 20 minutes away. We will be happy to advise guests on routes and can arrange transportation from stations and airports with prior notice.
