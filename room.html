Art Deco Suite

€325

Immerse yourself in the glamour of the 1920s! Boasting its original sycamore and birds-eye maple panelling and furnishings, carved stone fireplace and black marble and silver mosaic bathroom this spacious ( 70 m2 ) group of rooms will transport you straight back to the jazz age.

The suite has been enhanced with significant period artworks and the decadent bathroom has been sympathetically brought into the 21st century with the addition of a large whirlpool tub for two and walk in shower to allow for maximum enjoyment . A unique experience.
